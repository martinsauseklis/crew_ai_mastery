Subject: Innovation Planning Challenge Brief

Dear Teams,

We are excited to welcome you to our competitive innovation challenge aimed at extracting the best ideas from your diverse and talented teams. Below are the details, structure, and rules of the competition to ensure a smooth and productive process.

Competition Structure:
1. **Initial Proposal Round**: Teams will submit their initial plans based on the given topic within the specified timeframe.
2. **Feedback and Iteration Round**: Teams will receive feedback on their initial proposals and will have the opportunity to refine and improve their plans.
3. **Final Presentation Round**: Teams will submit their final plans and present their ideas in a forum to the judges.

Evaluation Criteria:
- **Innovativeness**: Originality and creativity of the plan.
- **Feasibility**: Practicality and implementability of the proposed solution.
- **Impact**: Potential effectiveness and scalability of the idea.
- **Clarity**: Precision and clarity of the presentation and documentation.

Three-Round Improvement Process:
- **Round 1: Initial Proposal**
  - Submit a comprehensive plan that outlines your approach, expected impact, and methodology.
- **Round 2: Feedback & Iteration**
  - Utilize feedback from judges to refine and enhance your initial plan.
- **Round 3: Final Submission**
  - Present a well-polished, thoroughly revised final plan to maximize impact and clarity.

Expectations and Deliverables:
- **Quality**: Ensure that each submission is thoroughly researched and thoughtfully presented.
- **Deadlines**: Adherence to all deadlines is crucial; late submissions may result in disqualification.
- **Collaboration**: Leverage the strengths of your team to enhance the innovation process.

We look forward to witnessing your innovative solutions and recognize all of your efforts and dedication. If you have any questions, please reach out.

Best of luck to all teams!

Sincerely,
The Coordination Team
